Addiction Services

bogart company offers a full continuum of addiction services that range from residential detoxification to traditional outpatient services.
All of the service components in the continuum welcome those who also have mental health disorders.
We believe that there should be no wrong door for individuals to access substance use disorder treatment.
With one call, we can guide you through the process of inquiry, assessment and admission to the most appropriate level of care.
We will assist you with understanding your insurance benefit,
help you understand what other sources of funding might be available and qualify you or your loved one for treatment. Our commitment is to treat you and your family with a welcoming heart,
provide compassion and understanding in time of need and to offer hope and
help through the provision of excellent care.
